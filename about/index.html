We are a US Based Fiber Optic Educational and Consulting Company. Mundo Interconnect LLC was founded in 2016 to educate, consult, design, and install optical telecommunication systems in the US, Latin America and Russia. We have 50 years of combined experience in Fiber Optic and Telecommunication Industry, which includes theoretical, design and hands on expertise. We have business relationships with multiple Global, US and European Fiber Optic Companies. We provide education and hands on training to students, professionals and any interested parties in fiber optic theory and optical components that are commonly used in telecommunication industry.

Mundo Interconnect es una empresa de consultoría técnica y educación de fibra óptica con sede en los Estados Unidos, formada por dos experimentados ingenieros de la industria; Andrei Vankov y Jose Mosquera. Fue fundada en 2016 con el propósito de proveer educación, consultoría, diseño e instalación de sistemas de telecomunicaciones ópticas en los Estados Unidos y América Latina. Mundo Interconnect consta con el permiso #365 otorgado por la Asociación de Fibra Optica (FOA) con base California-USA.
