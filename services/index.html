Mundo Interconnect

We provide education and hands on training to students and any interested parties in fiber optic theory and optical components that are commonly used in telecommunication industry such as Fiber to the Premises including copper datacom assemblies. FTTP (fiber-to-the-premises): where the fiber network includes both homes, businesses and cell towers. Broadband network architecture in which optical fiber is used to connect the remote radio head (RRH) to the base station in new antennas, or retrofitted in existing ones, to replace all or part of the coax local loop. We also provide training and US technical fiber optics certification for technicians that are looking to work in fiber optic and telecommunication industry. We carry a line of state of the art datacom equipment such as Fiber Connectors Termination Station, Optical Polishers, Interferometers, Network Analyzers, Optical Time Domain Reflectometers, Power Meters, Optical Microscopes and related consumables. We host business seminars, technical events, optical trade shows and HANDS ON trainig.
